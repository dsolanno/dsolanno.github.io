About Me

Research Scientist in Machine Learning

David, computer scientist by the Univerisity of Barcelona (UB) since 2013, started his career with a strong focus on software development, mostly oriented to the creation of proof of concepts for research projects.
Because of that, we gained experience as full-stack developer, having led the development of a technical architecture of a research project funded by the EU commission in collaboration with a consortium of Industrial and Academic partners.
Although he had a role mostly oriented to software engineering, he also participated in the design, development and writting of different research articles and patents accepted in the U.S. office in a variety of domains such as Smart Mobility, Data centers management or Crowdsourcing projects.
Because of his interest on Data Science, he studied a post-graduate course in Data Science (2017) by the University of Barcelona (UB) and a Msc in Data Science Foundations (2018) by the same university.
After, he pursued a PhD in the topic of Algorithmic Fairness at University Pompeu Fabra (UPF), which required deep knowledge of statistical and machine learning (ML) techniques. His work has been validated by peer-review, with different publications in top-rank conferences in the field. During this period, he also participated as associate professor in subjects related to Big Data development and Ethics in ML, training and improving his communication skills.
Given his background, David has proven to be capable to deliver good results in data science projects in a variety of domains. Being able to analyze large data volumes to stract insights, build advanced algorihtms and evaluate them, not only from the perspective of the owner, but also looking at the impact they might have for the end users. Additionally, we is used to communicate advance research results to the scientific community but also to multi-disciplinar collaborators with different levels of expertise in the field.
